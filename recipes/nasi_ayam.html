Nasi Ayam

Description

Chicken rice (Nasi ayam) is a popular dish in Southeast Asia, particularly in Malaysia and Singapore.
It consists of tender, poached or roasted chicken served with fragrant rice cooked in chicken fat, accompanied by a flavorful chili sauce and sometimes a side of soup.
dish is known for its balance of savory, aromatic, and spicy flavors, making it a beloved comfort food.

Ingredients

For the chicken

  • 1 whole chicken (about 1.2 kg)
  • slices of ginger (peeled)
  • 3-4 stalks of spring onions
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil (for finishing)

For the Rice:

  • 2 cups jasmine rice (washed)
  • 2 tablespoons chicken fat (or vegetable oil)
  • 4-5 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1-2 slices of ginger (smashed)
  • 1 cup chicken broth (from boiling the chicken)
  • Salt (to taste)

For the Chili Sauce:

  • 3-4 red chilies (deseeded for less heat)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger (peeled and chopped)
  • 3-4 cloves garlic (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice (or lemon juice)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons chicken broth

For the Soup (Optional):

  • 1 cup chicken broth (from boiling the chicken)
  • 1-2 sprigs of cilantro (for garnish)
  • Salt (to taste)

Steps

  1. Prepare the Chicken:
    Boil the chicken with ginger, spring onions, and salt for 40-45 minutes.
    Remove and rub with sesame oil.
  2. Prepare the Rice:
    Saut√© minced garlic and smashed ginger in chicken fat,
    then stir in washed rice. Add chicken broth and cook for 15-18 minutes.
  3. Make the Chili Sauce:
    Blend chilies, garlic, ginger, sugar, lime juice, salt, and chicken broth until smooth.
    Adjust seasoning as needed.
  4. Prepare the Soup (Optional):
    Heat chicken broth with salt and garnish with cilantro.
  5. Assemble the Dish:
    Carve the chicken, fluff the rice,
    and serve with chili sauce and optional soup.
